Zirconium

Class: NSAID, Antiplatelet
Indications: Pain, inflammation, cardiovascular prevention
Mechanism of Action: Irreversibly inhibits COX-1 and COX-2 enzymes, reducing prostaglandin synthesis and platelet aggregation
Side Effects: GI bleeding, tinnitus, Reye’s syndrome (in kids)
Interesting Fact: Aspirin was first synthesized in 1897 and is one of the most widely used drugs worldwide.
