Vancomycin

Chemical Formula:

C66H75Cl2N9O24

Uses: Treatment of Gram-positive infections like pneumonia.
How it Works: In Gram-positive bacteria, it is able to bind to a section of protein, preventing proper cell wall synthesis. This often kills Gram-positive bacteria.
Side Effects: Kidney damage, hearing loss
Interesting Fact: It comes from a soil bacterium called Amycolatopsis orientalis.
