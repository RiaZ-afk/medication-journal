Penicillin

Chemical Formula: C₁₆H₁₈N₂O₄S
Uses Strep throat, skin infections, pneumonia
How it Works: It binds to penicillin-binding proteins (originally named right?) on bacteria, which causes them to burst and die.
Side Effects: Rash, diarrhea, nausea
Interesting Fact: Peniccillin was the first widely used antibiotic.
