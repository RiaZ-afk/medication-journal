Tramadol

Chemical Formula: C₁₆H₂₅NO₂
Uses: Used for medium-level pain, is often used after surgery
How it Works: Binds to mu-opioid receptors in the brain, blocking pain.
Side Effects: Drowsiness, dizziness, nausea
Interesting Fact: Like morphine, people can become addicted to tramadol.
