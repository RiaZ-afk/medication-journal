Ursodiol

Chemical Formula: C₂₄H₄₀O₄
Uses: Improve bile flow, treat PBC (liver disease)
How it Works: It replaces more toxic acids in the liver.
Side Effects: Nausea, diarrhea
Interesting Fact: Ursodiol was originally extracted from bears.
Note: Ursa Major is also known as the Great Bear, and URSOdiol starts similarly to Ursa. I think they're related.
