Aspirin (Acetylsalicylic Acid)

Uses: Pain relief, anti-inflammation, reduces fever
Chemical Formula: C9H8O4
How it Works: Blocks an enzyme called cyclooxygenase (COX) which produces prostaglandin, which promotes pain, feverm and inflammation.
Side Effects: Toxic to liver in large doses; allergic reactions.
Interesting Fact: Aspirin comes from willow trees.
