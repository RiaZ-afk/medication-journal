Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)

Class: Analgesic, Antipyretic
Indications: Pain relief, fever reduction
Mechanism of Action: Inhibits prostaglandin synthesis in the CNS; acts on the hypothalamic heat-regulating center.
Side Effects: Liver toxicity in high doses
Interesting Fact: Paracetamol is often preferred over NSAIDs when stomach ulcers are a concern.
