Montelukast

Chemical Formula: C₃₅H₃₆ClNO₃S
Uses: Asthma, allergies
How it Works: Montelukast blocks D4 receptors. Leukotrienes, which cause inflammation and sneezing, need to bind to the D4 receptors to activate.
Side Effects: Headache, coughing, fever
Interesting Fact: It has mental side effects.
