Cetizirine

Chemical Formula:

C33H35FN2O5

Uses: High cholesterol
How it Works: It blocks the HMG-CoA reductase, which is an enzyme that creates cholesterol. When cholesterol production goes down, the liver pulls out more LDLs (bad cholesterol) out of the bloodstream.
Side Effects: Headache, diarrhea, upset stomach
Interesting Fact:Atorvastatin is one of the best-selling drugs ever.
