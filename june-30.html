Methylphenidate

Chemical Formula: C₁₄H₁₉NO₂
Uses: ADHD, narcolepsy (a sleep disorder)
How it Works: It prevents the absorption of dopamine and norepinephrine, which cause awareness.
Side Effects: Difficulty sleeping, anxiety
Interesting Fact: Although it is used to help people keep awake, it calms people with ADHD.
