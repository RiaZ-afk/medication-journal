Albuterol

Chemical Formula: C₁₃H₂₁NO₃
Uses: Asthma, COPD, bronchospasm (tight airways)
How it Works: It stimulates receptors that make the smooth muscles in the airways open up.
Side Effects: Nervousness, headache, cough, cramps, shivering
Interesting Fact: Albuterol is on the list of World Health Organization’s List of Essential Medicines.
