Insomnia

Overview: Insomnia is a sleeping disorder that makes it hard for people to go to sleep or stay asleep, which makes them tired.
Symptoms: Having trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or being tired during the day.
Causes: Stress, depression, too much coffee/screen time, naps, etc.
Types: Acute Insomnia (short-term) & Chronic Insomnia (long-term)
Treatments: Medications (not recommended), CBT-I, relaxation
