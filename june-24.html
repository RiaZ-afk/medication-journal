Paracetamol

Chemical Formula: C₈H₉NO₂
Uses: Headaches, muscle aches, arthritis, reduces fever
How it Works: Paracetamol blocks the COX enzyme, which then reduces the number of prostaglandins.
Side Effects: Nausea, rash, liver damage
Interesting Fact: Paracetamol is one of the most widely used medications in the world.
