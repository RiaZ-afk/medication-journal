Diphenhydramine

Chemical Formula: C₁₇H₂₁NO
Uses: Colds, insomnia, allergic reactions
How it Works: It blocks histamine, a chemical that causes itchiness and swelling.
Side Effects: Drowsiness, dizziness
Interesting Fact: Diphenhydramine, although used commonly for allergic reactions, can also be used as a sleeping aid.
