Amoxicillin

Chemical Formula: C₂₅₆H₃₈₁N₆₅O₇₉S₆
Uses: Type 1 & Type 2 Diabetes
How it Works: It allows sugar to enter cells, lowering the concentration of glucose in the blood.
Side Effects: Hypoglycemia (low blood sugar)
Interesting Fact: Insulin was originally extracted from dogs.
